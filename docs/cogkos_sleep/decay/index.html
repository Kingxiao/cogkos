Knowledge decay task

Implements time-based confidence decay for unconfirmed or unused knowledge. Formula: effective_confidence(t) = confidence × e^(-λt) Modified by activation_weight to slow decay for frequently accessed knowledge.

Mixed Decay Model:

  • Business knowledge: StepFunction (version replacement, no natural decay)
  • Experiential/Learned knowledge: ExponentialDecay
